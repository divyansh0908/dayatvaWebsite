A Walk to the Future

A Walk to the Future

Dayatva had conducted a rally – ‘A Walk to the Future’, on 3rd December, 2017 from 6.00AM to 8.00AM at Kankaria Lake, Ahmedabad. The rally witnessed a footfall of around 800 people from various parts of the city, where they walked together promoting the idea of educating the underprivileged. Many banners were displayed and slogans were hailed. An aerobics session was also conducted for all the members who had joined the rally, followed by a drawing competition open for all, with the theme ‘India 2050’. The event received a very positive response from the public, and significant media coverage.
