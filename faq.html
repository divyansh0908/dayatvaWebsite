Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What does Dayatva do?

Ans. You can visit our About section to know more about Dayatva.

Q. How can I become a member at Dayatva?

Ans. You can fill up this form.

Q. Who all can join Dayatva?

Ans. Currently, only students of IITRAM can become a member, though you can contribute in our initiative by Contacting Us.

Q. How can I be a volunteer at Dayatva?

Ans. Dayatva requires volunteers for conducting various social event or test in different schools. You can become a volunteer by filling up this form.

Q. Is teaching the only job I can do as a Dayatva member?

Ans. No, a Dayatva member has several other tasks such as the management of events and classes, content writing, social media handling etc.

Q. What are the social events organized at Dayatva?

Ans. Dayatva has organized - 'A Walk to the Future' and 'A Walk to the Future-2.0' to create awareness about various social causes, and will be organizing more such events in the future.

Q. How are students enrolled in Dayatva?

Ans. The Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation(AMC) provides students who have cleared the NMMS examination, so that they can get trained at Dayatava for NTSE, since these students are highly deserving, yet unfortunately lesser privileged to attain proper guidance. Additionally, Dayatva conducts DAATSE in various government schools across Ahmedabad to enroll bright students from these schools.

Q. What are the subjects taught at Dayatva?

Ans. Students are trained in various subjects such as Maths, Science, Social Sciences, English, Mental Ability - in both Hindi as well as in Gujarati.

Q. Students of which classes are enrolled at Dayatva?

Ans. At Dayatva, Students of class 8th, 9th and 10th are trained for NTSE as well as for their board examinations.
